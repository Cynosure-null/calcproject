The Telescoping Series Test


Purple dots are the divergent series \( \dfrac{1}{n}-n \), orange dots are the convergent series \( \dfrac{1}{n} - \dfrac{1}{n+1} \). The x's are their respective sums.

This test should be used when two fractions are present in a series. If the two fractions cancel each other out, then the series is convergent.
Steps:
  1. Split series into the form \( a_n - b_n \)
  2. Create a table of \( a_n \) and \( b_n \)
  3. Find if the two cancel each other out (for example if \( b_{n} = a_{n+1} \) will cancel)
If they cancel, the series is convergent
