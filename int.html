The Integral Test


Purple dots are the divergent series \(ln(n)\), orange dots are the convergent series \( \dfrac{1}{n^2} \). The x's are their respective sums.

This test involves using the integral of series \( a_n \). If the integral converges, the sum of the series converges and vice versa.

\( f(x) := a_n \\ \) \( \boxed{ \text{convergence of} \int_1^{\infty} f(x)dx \implies \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \text{converges}} \)
