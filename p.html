P-Series Test


Purple dots are the divergent series \( \dfrac{1}{n^{0.8}} \), orange dots are the convergent series \( \dfrac{1}{n^2} \). The x's are their respective sums.

Use this test when a series can be written in the form of \( \dfrac{1}{n^{p}} \). If \( p > 1\) the series converges

\( \boxed{ p>1 \implies \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \text{ converges}} \)
