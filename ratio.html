The Ratio Test


Purple dots are the divergent series \( \dfrac{2n}{n} \), orange dots are the convergent series \( \dfrac{2^n}{n!} \). The x's are their respective sums.

This test proves that if the ratio \( | \dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} |\) is less than 1, the series converges.
\( \boxed{|\dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}| < 1 \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \text{ diverges} } \)
