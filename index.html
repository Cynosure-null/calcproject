Series Tests





Nth term Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=1 \end{array} a_n \)

Geometric Series Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a(r^n) \)

Telescoping Series Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n - b_n \)

Integral Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \Rightarrow \int_{n}^{\infty} f(x)dx \)

P-Series Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=1 \end{array} \dfrac{1}{n^p} \)

Direct-Comparision Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n < \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} b_n \)

Limit-Comparison Test
\( \lim_{n\to\infty} \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n < \lim_{n\to\infty} \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} b_n \)

Alternating-Series Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} (-1)^{(n)}a_n \)

Ratio Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \Rightarrow |\dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}|\)

Root Test
\( \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \Rightarrow \sqrt[^n]{a_n} \)

Absolute Convergence Implies Convergence
