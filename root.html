The Root Test


Purple dots are the divergent series \( \frac{2^{n}}{n\sqrt{n}} \), orange dots are the convergent series \( 2^{-\frac{1}{n}} \). The x's are their respective sums.

This test states that if the \( \sqrt[^n]{|a_n|} \) is less than 1, the series converges and vice versa
\( \boxed{ \sqrt[^n]{|a_n|} > 1 \implies \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \text{ diverges} } \)
\( \boxed{ \sqrt[^n]{|a_n|} < 1 \implies \begin{array}{c} \infty \\ \sum \\ n=0 \end{array} a_n \text{ converges} } \)
